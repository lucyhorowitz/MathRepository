## Theorem
Let $\mathcal g$ be a finite-[[dimension of vector space|dimensional]] [[Lie algebra]] and let $\mathcal U(\mathfrak g)$ be its [[universal enveloping algebra]]. Then a $\mathfrak g$-[[module over a Lie algebra|module]] is [[irreducible module|irreducible]] if and only if its corresponding $\mathcal U(\mathfrak g)$-[[module over a Lie algebra|module]] is [[irreducible module|irreducible]].
