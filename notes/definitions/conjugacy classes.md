Let $G$ be a [[group]]. Then $G$ [[group action|acts]] on itself by [[inner automorphism of a group|conjugation]]: $$g\cdot x = gxg^{-1}.$$ The [[orbit|orbits]] of this action are called **conjugacy classes** of $G$.
