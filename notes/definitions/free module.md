Let $R$ be a [[ring]]. An $R$-[[module over a ring|module]] $M$ is **free** over a subset $S\subseteq M$ if $S$ is [[linearly independent]] and [[generate an R-module|generates]] $M$. We say that $S$ forms a **basis** for $M$.
