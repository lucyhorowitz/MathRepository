Let $(X,\Sigma,\mu)$ be a [[measure space]]. A set $A\subseteq \mathbb X$ is **d-open** or **open in the density topology** (or in the **Denjoy** topology) if 
1. $A$ is [[measurable]]
2. The [[measure density]] $d(A,x) = 1$ for every $x \in A$.
