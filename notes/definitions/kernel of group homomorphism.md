Let $G$ and $H$ be [[group|groups]]. The **kernel** of a [[group homomorphism]] $f:G\to H$ is the set of all elements $g\in G$ such that $f(g) = e_H$, i.e. the elements of $G$ that get mapped to the [[identity element]] in $H$.
