## Theorem
Let $V$ be a finite-dimensional [[definitions/vector space]], and $W \subset V$ a [[vector subspace]]. Then the dimension of the [[quotient vector space]] $V/W$ is $$\text{dim}(V/W) - \text{dim}(V)-\text{dim}(W).$$
