## Theorem
Let $L$ be a [[formal language of propositional logic]]. Thenf or any $\phi,\psi\in \text{Form}(L)$, the negation of $(\phi\rightarrow \psi)$ [[syntactic proof|proves]] the negation of $\psi$ and it [[syntactic proof|proves]] $\phi$.
