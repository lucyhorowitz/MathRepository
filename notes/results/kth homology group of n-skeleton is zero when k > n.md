## Theorem
Let $X$ be a [[CW complex]]. Then the $k$th [[homology group]] of the $n$-skeleton $X^n$ is trivial if $k >n$. That is, $$H_k(X^n) = 0 \text{ if } k > n.$$ In particular, for $X$ finite-dimensional, $H_k(X) = 0$ for $k > \text{dim}(X)$.
