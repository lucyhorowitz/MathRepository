Let $\mathfrak g$ be a [[Lie algebra]] and let $(V,\rho_V)$ be a [[Lie algebra representation|representation]] of $\mathfrak g$. Then the **[[dual]] representation** of $\mathfrak g$ is the pair $(V^*,\rho_{V^*})$ where $V^*$ is the [[dual space]] of $V$ and $\rho_{V^*}$ is given by $$\rho_{V^*}(x) = -\rho_V(x)^
*$$ for all $x \in \mathfrak g$.
