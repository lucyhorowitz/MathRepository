## Theorem
An [[inner product space]] $(V,\langle\cdot,\cdot\rangle)$ is also a [[normed linear space]] with the [[norm]] of a vector $v$ given by the square root of the [[inner product]] of $V$ with itself: $$||v|| = \sqrt{\langle v,v\rangle}.$$
